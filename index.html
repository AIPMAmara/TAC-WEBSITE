ABOUT THE AMAZON CONFERENCE

Raising Women Who Will Shape the Future

The future needs more women in boardrooms, government offices, technology hubs, financial institutions, classrooms, and executive leadership. But representation does not happen by chance. It is earned through preparation, competence, confidence, and consistent growth.

Nigeria's population is almost evenly split between men and women, yet across industries there remains a significant gap between the need for capable female leaders and the number of women prepared to step into positions of influence. The challenge is not simply access to opportunity, it is readiness when opportunity arrives.

The Amazon Conference was created to bridge this gap.

More than a conference, it is a leadership development movement designed to equip young African women with the mindset, skills, networks, and exposure required to thrive in leadership, entrepreneurship, governance, academia, technology, and the corporate world.

Through transformative conversations, practical leadership training, mentorship, and direct access to accomplished female leaders, participants gain the clarity to define their vision, the confidence to pursue it, and the competence to execute it.

We are raising women who do not wait to be invited into the future. Women who create value, lead with excellence, seize opportunities, and leave a lasting impact wherever they find themselves.

The future will belong to those who prepare for it. The Amazon Conference is where that preparation begins.

ROADMAP & TIMELINE

Your Journey to Becoming an Amazon

Follow the transformational leadership journey from registration to mentorship, designed to equip you with the skills, confidence, and network required to thrive as a leader in today's world.

1

Phase 1: Registration

Registration Opens

The registration portal officially opens on 1st July, 2026 for ambitious young women under the age of 30 who are ready to invest in their personal growth, leadership development, and future impact.

2

Phase 2: Deadline

Registration Closes

Registration closes on 14th August, 2026. Participation is granted on a first-come, first-served basis, and interested delegates are encouraged to secure their spots early.

3

Phase 3: The Amazon Conference 2026

Main Conference Day

The Amazon Conference holds on 15th August, 2026 in Abakaliki, Ebonyi State, bringing together young women from diverse backgrounds for a powerful day of leadership training, personal development, mentorship, networking, and transformational conversations.

4

Phase 4: Mentorship

Post-Conference Growth Journey

The conference experience does not end when the event closes. Delegates gain access to post-conference mentorship, accountability, leadership resources, and a supportive community designed to help them translate insights into action and sustain their growth journey.

5

Phase 5: Leadership Action

From Learning to Impact

Participants are encouraged to apply the lessons learned through personal, academic, professional, entrepreneurial, and community leadership initiatives. This phase focuses on turning inspiration into measurable impact.

6

Phase 6: Community

Join the Amazon Network

Delegates become part of a growing network of young women committed to excellence, leadership, and influence. Through continued engagement, collaboration, and opportunities, Amazons remain connected long after the conference concludes.
